# General Template

Use this as the base Chinese template for event registration, project application, innovation award, startup competition, roadshow, or partnering forms. Replace bracketed placeholders. Delete modules that do not apply.

## Checkbox Text

### Mandatory General Checkbox

> 我已阅读并同意[个人信息处理者名称]按照《个人信息处理告知》处理我提交的个人信息。

### Mandatory Cross-Border Checkbox

Use only when personal information is provided to or accessed by an overseas recipient.

> 我同意[个人信息处理者名称]将我提交的相关信息提供给境外接收方[境外接收方名称]，用于[具体目的]。

### Optional Marketing Checkbox

Use only when the handler will send later activity invitations, cooperation opportunities, or industry updates beyond the immediate activity.

> 我同意接收[个人信息处理者名称]发送的类似活动、合作机会或行业资讯，并知道我可以随时退订。

## Field Mapping Notes

Common fields:

- name: 姓名
- job title: 职务
- company/project name: 公司/机构名称、项目名称
- city: 所在城市
- email: 电子邮箱
- mobile phone number: 手机号码
- therapeutic area: 专业领域 or 治疗领域
- therapeutic modality: 技术类型 or 治疗技术类型
- partnering or competition intention: 合作或参赛意向
- project name/summary/stage/direction/materials: 项目名称、项目简介、项目阶段、合作方向、项目材料
- pitch deck or BP: 项目材料 or 商业计划书

When the exact form fields are visible, mirror them exactly in the information categories sentence. If some fields are unclear from screenshots, say they need confirmation instead of inventing the label.

## Cross-Border Module Checklist

Before including a separate overseas transfer checkbox, confirm:

- overseas recipient name
- recipient contact method
- purpose
- processing method, such as viewing, evaluating, necessary saving, and contacting
- information categories
- rights route or procedure

Do not insist on the recipient country/region as a standalone legal requirement. Include it when useful for transparency or when the client already knows it.
